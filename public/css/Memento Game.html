Memento: The Video Memory Game

Instructions

Indicate which videos you remember by playing a simple memory game on short 3 second videos!

You will watch a stream of 3-second videos. Your task is to tell us when you see a video we've already shown.

Guidelines:

  1. Press the play button to start the video sequence.
  2. Press SPACEBAR when you see a video that has been shown already.
  3. If your accuracy is good, you will be able to advance to the next level!
